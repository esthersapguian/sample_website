AREA 3 CONVENTION 2025

Free Believers in Christ Fellowship Inc.

Photo courtesy of LuBiC

Join us for a powerful gathering of believers as we come together in worship, fellowship, and celebration. This convention will be a time of spiritual renewal, where we embrace God's presence and His divine purpose for our lives. Experience transformative teachings, inspiring testimonies, and moments of deep prayer that will strengthen your faith and ignite your passion for Christ.

Our Vision

To be a community of believers passionately pursuing God's presence, transforming lives through His love, and impacting our world with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

To equip and empower believers to live out their faith authentically, serve with excellence, and spread the message of hope and salvation to every generation.
